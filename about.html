About

Me

Welcome to my blog about the craft of software development, coding and including some career advice. I've been developing full-time for about 8 years, the last 5 years as a freelancer, though I created my first websites (with Perl!) in 1998. I've built a couple of side businesses, one of them is profitable (a niche jobboard), but my time is mostly spent as a freelance developer / software architect / consultant.

I live in Germany and my stack has included PHP (plain, Laravel, Symfony), JavaScript (jQuery, Vue.js, Angular, TypeScript), Python (Flask and hopefully soon Django), Docker, AWS and some other buzzwords. I mostly worked with very small to medium-sized companies, sometimes at their office, sometimes remote or in my home office.

Blog

This blog started »by hand«, i.e. I didn't use a generator a backend programming language or a JavaScript framework, but rather just some plain html and some styles and a font or two. I was thinking of using Hugo, or Jekyll, or even Next.js or maybe even something exotic. But in the end, I wanted something hassle-free.

Yes, there will be copy-and-pasting involved, but that just motivates me to think things through before I add something new :-). And to be strategic about changes.
